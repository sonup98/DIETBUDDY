# 🥗 NutriTrack AI

A modern, intelligent, and user-friendly nutrition tracking application built with **Flutter** that helps users achieve their health and fitness goals by tracking calories, macronutrients, micronutrients, water intake, and weight progress.

Unlike traditional calorie-tracking apps, **NutriTrack AI** is designed to make logging homemade meals simple. Users can enter ingredients and quantities, and the app automatically calculates nutritional values while tracking daily progress toward personalized fitness goals.

# 🎯 Project Goal

NutriTrack AI aims to simplify nutrition tracking by allowing users to log foods using ingredients instead of complicated calorie counting. The app automatically calculates nutritional values, tracks daily goals, provides intelligent insights, and helps users make healthier decisions through an intuitive and visually appealing experience.

Whether your goal is losing fat, building muscle, maintaining weight, or simply eating healthier, NutriTrack AI is designed to be your all-in-one nutrition companion.
